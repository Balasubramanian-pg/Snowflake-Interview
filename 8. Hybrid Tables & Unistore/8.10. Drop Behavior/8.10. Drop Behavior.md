# Snowflake Drop Behavior

**Description:**
In Snowflake, dropping an object does not immediately delete it. Instead, it removes the object's metadata, and the data remains recoverable within a specified retention period. This allows for safe deletion with a recovery window, controlled cleanup of environments, protection against accidental drops, and lifecycle management of data objects.
