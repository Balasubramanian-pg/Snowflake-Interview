# Snowflake AT and BEFORE: Precision Time Travel Querying

**Description:**
The AT and BEFORE clauses in Snowflake Time Travel are direct entry points into Snowflake's metadata timeline, letting you choose when in history to materialize a dataset. AT retrieves a table state at a specific timestamp or offset, while BEFORE retrieves the table state just before a given query ID.
