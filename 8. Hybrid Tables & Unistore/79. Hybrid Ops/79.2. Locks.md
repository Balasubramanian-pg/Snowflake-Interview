# Locks in Snowflake: Synchronization Mechanisms for Data Integrity

**Description:**
Locks play a crucial role in multi-threaded environments, preventing data corruption and ensuring that shared resources are accessed safely. They can be classified into different types, such as mutual exclusion locks (mutexes), read-write locks, and spin locks. Locks must be released after use to avoid deadlocks and ensure other threads can access the shared resource.
