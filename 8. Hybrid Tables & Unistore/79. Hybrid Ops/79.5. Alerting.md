# Alerting in Hybrid Ops

**Description:**
Alerting in Hybrid Ops involves designing and implementing a system that triggers notifications for critical events, such as system failures or performance degradation. The goal is to provide clear, actionable information to facilitate swift response and minimize downtime. A good alerting system balances notification frequency and relevance to avoid alert fatigue.
