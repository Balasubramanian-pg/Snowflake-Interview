# Dropping Hybrid Indexes in Snowflake

**Description:**
Dropping a hybrid index in Snowflake refers to the act of deleting an index that was previously created on a hybrid table. This process is essential for maintaining optimal performance and managing storage resources effectively. When a hybrid index is dropped, Snowflake removes the index structure and the associated metadata, freeing up storage space.
