# Resets in System Management

**Description:**
Resets in system management refer to the process of restoring a system, application, or component to a known good state. This is essential for ensuring reliability, minimizing downtime, and preventing data loss or security vulnerabilities. Resets can be hardware-based, software-based, or a combination of both.
